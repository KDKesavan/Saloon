About Us

The Journey of Sarvesh and His Passion for Hair!

I’m Sarvesh, and I’ve been passionate about the world of hairstyling and grooming for over 12 years. It all started back in 2019 when I decided to follow in my father's footsteps—he's been in this business for years, and I grew up watching him work his magic.

After finishing my B.Sc. in Chemistry. My goal has always been to build strong connections, not just with hair but with people too. So, here I am, continuing the family tradition with my own twist, helping my clients look and feel great every day

Since 2019

Sarvesh has been dedicated to creating a unique salon experience. With a focus on quality and personal connections, he ensures every client leaves feeling their best.
